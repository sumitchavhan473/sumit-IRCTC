Product Description

Alluring Perfumed Talc

Let the soft caress of Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Talc pamper your skin. This Talcum Powder for women is a sweet symphony of Passionfruit Flowers, Roses and Vanilla. We've made it with fine grade powder that is easy to apply on both the body, and the face. So you can enjoy a radiant, even-toned look while your fragrance captivates that special someone.

Use it right after a shower, after you've applied the Enchanteur Alluring Body Lotion. Take a little powder in your hands and dab it all over. Blend it into your skin for the desired look.

Alluring Perfumed Roll-on Deo

Enjoy the fresh and feminine feel of Enchanteur Alluring for longer, with this Roll-on Deo. Designed to offer lasting odor-protection, this Roll-on Deo for women is also infused with our signature Alluring Perfume. The fragrance notes of Passionfruit Flowers, Roses and Vanilla have been woven together with French perfume-making techniques. Try it and see its allure unfurl.

For best results, use it right after a shower, on freshly moisturized skin. Allow it to dry a bit before wearing your clothes.

Learn to layer

Alluring Perfumed Talc

The secret allure of Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Talc lies in layering. That means, we recommend you use one, or more, or all the products from the collection to accentuate the fragrance and create a stunning impression.

Before you use this Talcum Powder, have a shower and wipe yourself dry. Keep your skin’s after-bath glow alive by using Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Body Lotion right away. Take special care of the dry areas near your elbows, knees and ankles. To stay fresh and to get odor-protection, use the Enchanteur Alluring Roll-on Deo or Deo Spray. Then use Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Talc. Get silky smooth, even-toned skin. Finally, to seal in the fragrance, and cast your spell, spritz on the Enchanteur Alluring Perfume on your pulse points, like the inner sides of your wrists and elbows, behind your ears, and the base of your neck. You can also give a quick spritz in your freshly washed hair.

Go on now, make an unforgettable impression!

Alluring Perfumed Roll-on Deo

Love the fragrance of Enchanteur Alluring? Layer it up with other products in this collection to create a stunning impression.

After a shower, apply the Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Body Lotion. Lock in the moisture of your freshly cleansed skin. Take special care of the dry areas near your elbows, knees and ankles. Then use the Enchanteur Alluring Roll-on Deo for extra freshness and odor-protection. Follow this up, with Enchanteur Alluring Perfumed Talc for silky smooth, even-toned skin. Finally, to seal in the fragrance, and cast your spell, spritz on the Enchanteur Alluring Perfume on your pulse points, like the inner sides of your wrists and elbows, behind your ears, and the base of your neck. You can also spray some into your freshly shampooed hair.

Go on now, make an unforgettable impression!
